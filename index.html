LEHIGH UNIVERSITY

Welcome to Lehigh University, a dynamic and prestigious institution where academic excellence meets a vibrant community. Located in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania, Lehigh combines the resources of a research university with the close-knit feel of a smaller campus. Here, students are encouraged to explore their passions, challenge themselves intellectually, and make lasting connections with peers and faculty.

At Lehigh, you'll find a diverse range of programs across engineering, business, arts and sciences, and education, designed to prepare students for success in their careers and beyond. With state-of-the-art facilities and a strong emphasis on interdisciplinary learning, Lehigh offers a hands-on, real-world approach to education. Our dedicated faculty and supportive campus environment ensure that every student has the opportunity to thrive both inside and outside the classroom.

Whether you're participating in cutting-edge research, engaging in student organizations, or enjoying the scenic beauty of our campus, Lehigh is a place where you can grow, innovate, and make an impact. We invite you to discover all that Lehigh has to offer and become part of a tradition of excellence and leadership. Welcome to the Mountain Hawk family!
